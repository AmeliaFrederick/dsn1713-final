About

Who we are

Welcome to Toronto Cupcake! Founded in 2010, we are proud to be Toronto's leading destination for gourmet branded cupcakes. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, hosting a corporate event, or simply indulging in a sweet treat, our handcrafted cupcakes are made fresh daily using the finest ingredients. Explore our wide range of classic and seasonal flavours, and enjoy fast, reliable delivery across the GTA.

What we do

At Toronto Cupcake, we specialize in crafting delicious, handcrafted cupcakes that bring joy to every occasion. Our menu features a variety of flavours, from classic favourites to unique seasonal creations, all made with high-quality ingredients. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, or simply indulging in a sweet treat, our cupcakes are perfect for any moment. Place an order and discover your new favourite flavour!
